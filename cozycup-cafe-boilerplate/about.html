About COZYCUP

Our Story

Founded in 2025, COZYCUP began with a simple dream: to create a warm and welcoming space where coffee lovers could enjoy exceptional brews in a comfortable atmosphere.

Our commitment to quality starts with sourcing the finest coffee beans from sustainable farms and extends to our carefully crafted beverages and freshly baked pastries.
